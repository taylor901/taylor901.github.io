LSU AMB Associate Class of 2018

THEIR JOURNEY.

Our Associates have gone through a long journey to finally receive their yellow polos. Their journey started started with with a simple application then only got harder from there. Two selection proceses and an interview later we welcomed our 90 new associates. Being selected was only the start, months of training and tests is what they needed to complete before finally getting their polos.

Training

Our associates spent three months in traing, three months of early mornings, thats three monts of dedication. From 11am to 6pm ever Sunday they practices and learned new tools to be the best ambassadors they can be.

Training Trio

Naomi Westbrook was 2018 Training chair and was assisted by her Associate Chairs Taylor M Sanders and Anthony Doyle. All three of them are junior and were inducted into Ambassadors in 2016.

After Training

After Training the Associates were inducted into LSU Amassadors at the Yellow Polo Ceramony. Each Associate's polo was presented to them by their big buddy. Our Associates are now official Ambassadors after the ceramony.
